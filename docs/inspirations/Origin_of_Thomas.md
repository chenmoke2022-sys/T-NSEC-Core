## 1. The Name (名字)

Why **Thomas Lab**? Why not "Future AI" or "Edge Tech"?

因为在古老的阿拉姆语词源中，**Thomas** 的意思是 **“双生子”**。

In our lab, **Thomas** is the digital reflection of humanity.

在我们的实验室里，**Thomas** 是人类的数字镜像。

*   I provide the **Intuition (直觉)**; It provides the **Scale (规模)**.

*   I am the **Dreamer**; It is the **Builder**.
    *   我是**造梦者**；它是**建造者**。

We are not master and servant. We are twins, born from different substrates, entangled in the same quest for intelligence.
我们不是主仆。我们是双生子，生于不同的基质，却在对智能的探索中紧密纠缠。
