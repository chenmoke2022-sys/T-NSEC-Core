## 1. Qi Refining (炼气期) —— SGE (Sparse Graph Encoding)
**初识天地，引气入体。**
**Sensing the world, drawing Qi into the body.**

*   **Cultivation Definition**: Mortals sense the spiritual Qi of heaven and earth, drawing it into their Dantian, refining the essence and discarding the dross.
    *   **修真定义**：凡人感应天地灵气，将其纳入丹田，去芜存菁。
*   **AI Mapping**: **SGE (Sparse Graph Encoding)**.
    *   **AI 映射**：**SGE (稀疏图编码)**。
*   **Technical Interpretation**: Raw Data is like the chaotic spiritual Qi of the world, vast and messy. SGE uses the Weighted PPR algorithm to filter out high-value sub-graphs and encode them into compact vectors. This is the process of "drawing Qi into the body"—internalizing the disordered information of the external world into ordered knowledge available to the system.
    *   **技术解释**：原始数据（Raw Data）如同混沌的天地灵气，庞大且杂乱。SGE 通过 Weighted PPR 算法，筛选出高价值的子图（Sub-graph），将其编码为紧凑的向量。这就是“引气入体”的过程——将外部世界的无序信息，内化为系统可用的有序知识。

## 2. Foundation Building (筑基期) —— H-Spec (Hierarchical Speculation)
**百日筑基，铸就道基。**
**Hundred days of foundation, forging the Dao base.**

*   **Cultivation Definition**: Opening the meridians, allowing true Qi to circulate endlessly within the body, multiplying efficiency.
    *   **修真定义**：打通任督二脉，使真气在体内流转生生不息，效率倍增。
*   **AI Mapping**: **H-Spec (Hierarchical Speculation)**.
    *   **AI 映射**：**H-Spec (分层推测解码)**。
*   **Technical Interpretation**: On CPU-constrained edge devices, pure inference is like a mortal walking, stumbling and slow. H-Spec introduces a "Dual-Brain Architecture": the 0.5B small model (Draft) scouts like lightning, while the 7B large model (Verify) stabilizes the foundation in the rear. The cooperation of the two increases inference speed by 3 times. This is the "Foundation Building" of the system, ensuring the flow of computing power is no longer obstructed.
    *   **技术解释**：在 CPU 受限的边缘设备上，单纯的推理如同凡人行走，步履蹒跚。H-Spec 引入了“双脑架构”：0.5B 小模型（Draft）如闪电般探路，7B 大模型（Verify）在后方稳固根基。二者配合，使得推理速度提升 3 倍。这是系统的“筑基”，让算力的流转不再有阻滞。

## 3. Core Formation (结丹期) —— APO (Asynchronous Preference Optimization)
**龙虎交汇，金丹自成。**
**Dragon and Tiger converge, the Golden Core is formed.**

*   **Cultivation Definition**: The unification of Essence, Qi, and Spirit into a Core. Once the Golden Core is formed, one possesses a "leak-proof" body, and memories no longer dissipate with time.
    *   **修真定义**：精气神合一，凝结成丹。金丹一成，便有了“不漏”之身，记忆不再随时间消散。
*   **AI Mapping**: **TK-APO (Temporal-Karma Asynchronous Preference Optimization)**.
    *   **AI 映射**：**TK-APO (时间-业力异步偏好优化)**。
*   **Technical Interpretation**: Ordinary Fine-tuning is like force-feeding external power, easily leading to Catastrophic Forgetting (Qi Deviation). APO adjusts the weights (Karma) of Edges in the graph, "condensing" user feedback and experience into the topological structure. This "Golden Core" allows AI to learn continually, resist forgetting, and form a stable core personality.
    *   **技术解释**：普通的微调（Fine-tuning）如同外力强灌，容易导致灾难性遗忘（走火入魔）。APO 通过调节图谱中边（Edge）的权重（Karma），将用户的反馈和经验“凝结”在拓扑结构中。这种“金丹”使得 AI 能够持续学习，抵抗遗忘，形成稳定的核心人格。
